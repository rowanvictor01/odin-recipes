Sweet Filipino Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta of Italian origin, typically made from durum wheat semolina and water. It's a staple food in many cuisines, known for its versatility in pairing with various sauces, from simple tomato sauces to richer, meat-based options. Originally made by hand, its mass production became possible with the invention of mechanical extrusion presses, leading to its widespread popularity. Perfect food to prepare for birthdays and other related special events.

Cooking Time!

  1. Gather you ingredients if you haven't already

    We’ve got our tomato sauce, tomato paste, hot dogs, veggies and aromatics, and more, all ready to go!

  2. Cook and drain your pasta

    Any standard Spaghetti pasta is all you need here. Cook it according to package directions. Set aside.

  3. Mince the veggies and aromatics

    Place the garlic, onion, and oil in a large saucepan or skillet. Sautė for 3 - 4 minutes or until soft and fragrant. Add the ground pork and brown the meat until it's completely cooked.

  4. Sautė ground sausage, hot dogs, and veggies in a wok and combine everything

    Add the minced vegetables and hot dog pieces and stir to combine. Add the water and allow the mixture to simmer for 10 - 15 minutes. Add the tomato sauce and tomate paste to the meat and continue to simmer the mixture for another 10 - 15 minutes. Add the evaporated milk, salt, and seasoning mix and stir until incorporated.

  5. Mix pasta and sauce

    We’re almost there! Combine the pasta and the sauce in a large pot or mixing bowl. Top with grated cheese.

  6. Ready to serve

    Sweet, sticky, saucy perfection!
